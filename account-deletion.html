Delete Your Ummah Account

You can request deletion of your Ummah account and associated data at any time.

Option A (in-app):

  1. Open the Ummah app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Select Delete Account and confirm

If your current build doesn’t show “Delete Account” yet, use Option B below.

Option B (email request):

  1. Email us at privacy@ummah.app
  2. Use the subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. In the email, include the email address you used in Ummah

What gets deleted

  • Your account (authentication record) and profile details
  • Your uploaded content (videos) and related metadata (captions, categories)
  • Your interactions such as likes, bookmarks, follows, comments, and reports tied to your account

Retention

We aim to complete deletion within 30 days of a verified request. Some data may be retained longer if required for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or backup integrity, and will be deleted as soon as reasonably possible.
